Service Level Agreement (SLA)

1. Service Scope

Whoknows is a search platform that allows users to:

  • Search a wide range of topics, similar to standard internet search engines.
  • Create personal user accounts to log in and manage their profile.

The platform currently provides search functionality and basic account management only. There are no different account types, premium tiers, or additional services at this time.

External API access (such as weather or other data services) is not available under this agreement but may be considered for future development.

The service is globally accessible with no regional restrictions, and there are no enforced usage limits for users.

2. Performance Metrics

The Whoknows platform is intended to remain continuously available for the duration of the project period, which runs until June 26. While no formal uptime percentage is guaranteed, the service is expected to operate without interruption throughout this timeframe.

There are currently no scheduled maintenance windows, and the platform does not enforce any response time guarantees. However, every reasonable effort will be made to maintain a responsive and stable experience for users.

3. Response Time

The Whoknows platform does not provide any formal guarantees regarding response times for searches, logins, or other user actions. However, the team actively monitors performance and aims to provide a responsive experience.

As part of ongoing performance efforts:

  • The landing page is targeted to load within approximately 500 milliseconds under normal conditions.
  • Other pages on the site aim to respond within approximately 200 milliseconds.

These figures are goals rather than commitments, and actual performance may vary depending on network conditions and other external factors.

4. Security Measures

Whoknows is committed to maintaining a secure environment for its users. The following measures are in place to help protect data and user accounts:

  • All data transmitted between users and the platform is secured using HTTPS (SSL/TLS encryption) to ensure safe transit.
  • User passwords are securely hashed before being stored, following best practices to prevent unauthorized access.

While Whoknows implements technical safeguards, users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account credentials and ensuring their accounts remain secure (for example, by choosing strong, unique passwords).

At this time, Whoknows does not maintain formal backup procedures for data recovery.

5. Compliance Standards

Whoknows collects only the minimum data necessary for users to create and access an account. This includes a username, email address, and password. No personal names or sensitive personal data are collected.

User data is used solely for the purpose of enabling login functionality and account access. Data is not sold, shared, or used for any other purposes.

While Whoknows does not currently fall under any specific legal compliance frameworks such as GDPR or CCPA, it aims to follow basic principles of user privacy and responsible data handling.

Users are responsible for ensuring that their use of the platform complies with any applicable local laws or institutional policies that may apply to them.
